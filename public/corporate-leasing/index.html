Corporate & Relocation Leasing

Housing a team in Greater Cleveland? Tell us what your employees or relocating staff need and we'll see how we can help with local rental homes.

If your company needs to house relocating employees, project staff, or new hires in Greater Cleveland, tell us the details — number of people, timing, neighborhoods, and budget — and we'll let you know how our available rental homes can help. Every home we work with welcomes Housing Choice Vouchers as well.

Relocating people to Cleveland — new hires, project crews, traveling staff, or transferring employees — is easier with a local contact who knows the market and the neighborhoods. Instead of piecing together listings, send us your team's requirements and we'll tell you what's realistic from our current selection of Greater Cleveland rental homes.

We're a local rental team, not a national relocation vendor, so we'll be straight with you about what we can and can't do. If we're a fit, we'll help with available homes and coordinate showings; if your needs are outside what we work with, we'll say so.

Frequently asked questions

Do you provide furnished or short-term corporate housing?
We're a local rental team, and availability varies. Rather than over-promise, tell us exactly what you need — furnished vs. unfurnished, length of stay, number of people, and neighborhoods — and we'll tell you honestly what our current selection can and can't cover.
Can you house multiple employees at once?
Sometimes, depending on current availability. Send us the headcount, timing, and preferred areas and we'll let you know what's realistic from the homes we work with right now.
What areas of Greater Cleveland do you cover?
Our selection is concentrated on Cleveland's East and Southeast sides, with additional homes in nearby suburbs and cities like Akron, Lorain, and Elyria. Tell us where your team needs to be and we'll tell you what's available nearby.
