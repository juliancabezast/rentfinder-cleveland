What extended-stay housing looks like with a local rental team

Extended-stay usually means a month or longer — longer than a hotel booking, but shorter or more flexible than a standard year lease. If you're arranging a stay for a visiting consultant, a rotating project team, or new staff who haven't settled yet, a residential rental can be a more comfortable and lower-cost option than weeks in a hotel.

It helps to be clear about who we are up front. Rent Finder Cleveland is a local rental team — not a national relocation vendor or a corporate-housing chain. We help renters find, tour, and apply for homes across Greater Cleveland, and we can do the same when a company is placing staff. We don't run a hotel desk, and most homes we work with are standard unfurnished residential rentals leased on conventional terms.

Tell us three things: dates, headcount, and location

The fastest way for us to be useful is a short brief. With your dates, how many people you're housing, and roughly where they need to be, we can tell you quickly what our current selection can and can't cover — no drawn-out intake process.

  • Dates — earliest move-in and how long the stay runs (one month, three months, or open-ended)
  • Headcount — how many people, and whether they share one home or need separate homes
  • Location anchor — the office, job site, hospital, or campus they need to be near
  • Bedrooms and occupancy — bedrooms per home and total number of occupants
  • Must-haves — parking, ground-floor access, on-site laundry, or room to work
  • Furnished or unfurnished — and whether your team brings their own furniture

Where our current selection is concentrated

Being honest about geography saves everyone time. Our roughly 90 homes are concentrated on Cleveland's East and Southeast sides, with additional homes in some suburbs and in the Akron, Lorain, and Elyria areas. If your team needs to be inside a downtown tower, we may be a poor fit; if they're working near a facility or job site on the East side or in an outer community, we may have exactly what you need.

You can get a feel for typical layouts and areas on our houses for rent in Cleveland listings before you send a brief, so your request is grounded in what's realistic for the area.

What we can and can't cover for month-plus stays

We'd rather set expectations early than overpromise — here's the honest breakdown.

  • We can shortlist voucher-friendly, HUD-inspection-ready residential homes, arrange tours, and walk an application through to lease.
  • We can look at month-plus stays where a landlord we work with is open to a shorter or flexible term — tell us the length and we'll check.
  • We can't guarantee furnished units, housewares, bundled utilities, or hotel-style services; most homes are leased unfurnished.
  • We can't act as a relocation-management company handling flights, per diem, or nationwide placement — we're local to Greater Cleveland.
  • We won't know what fits until you share the dates and location, because availability changes week to week.

Local rules that affect longer stays

Every home we work with welcomes Housing Choice Vouchers (Section 8) and is kept ready for a CMHA Housing Quality Standards inspection — the same baseline that tends to make a home comfortable for a longer stay. If anyone on your team uses a voucher, that's already built in; you can read how to apply for Section 8 with CMHA for the steps.

The City of Cleveland does not currently have a source-of-income ordinance, though several inner-ring suburbs do — including Cleveland Heights, South Euclid, University Heights, Warrensville Heights, and Linndale. Whoever your team is, we follow the Fair Housing Act in every interaction and match homes strictly to your stated needs — bedrooms, occupancy, timing, and location — never to anything else.

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer furnished corporate apartments?
Usually not. Most homes we work with are standard unfurnished residential rentals. If furnished space is a must, tell us up front and we'll be candid about whether our current selection fits or whether a dedicated corporate-housing provider would serve you better.
What's the shortest stay you can arrange?
It depends on the individual landlord. Our niche is month-plus residential leases rather than nightly or weekly stays, so send your dates and we'll check which homes we work with are open to a shorter or flexible term.
Can you house a team in more than one home at once?
Often, yes, if availability lines up. Share your headcount and whether people can share, and we'll tell you what's open in the area you need.
Which areas can you actually cover?
Our roughly 90 homes are concentrated on Cleveland's East and Southeast sides, plus some suburbs and the Akron, Lorain, and Elyria areas. If your team needs to be downtown, we may not be the right fit — we'll say so.
Is there a fee to get a shortlist?
