Getting to the VA hospital by RTA

The HealthLine — GCRTA's bus-rapid-transit line along Euclid Avenue — runs directly through University Circle on a dedicated median busway, connecting Public Square downtown to East Cleveland and making it the most direct transit option to the VA medical campus without a car. The Red Line and the Blue/Green light-rail lines also converge at Tower City downtown for connections from other parts of the city.

Section 8, VASH vouchers, and renting near the VA

Veterans using a HUD-VASH voucher — which pairs a standard Housing Choice Voucher with VA case-management support — go through the same local voucher system as any other Section 8 applicant. Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA) administers vouchers for Cuyahoga County, with preliminary applications accepted year-round through CMHA's online portal. Every home Rent Finder Cleveland works with accepts Housing Choice Vouchers and is HUD-inspection-ready. Keep in mind Ohio has no statewide source-of-income law and Cleveland itself doesn't currently protect voucher holders from discrimination — see our Section 8 guide and our VASH voucher explainer for the full picture.

What is a HUD-VASH voucher?
HUD-VASH pairs a standard Housing Choice Voucher with VA case-management support for veterans experiencing or at risk of homelessness. It's administered locally the same way as regular Section 8 vouchers, through Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA), so the same application and inspection process applies.
