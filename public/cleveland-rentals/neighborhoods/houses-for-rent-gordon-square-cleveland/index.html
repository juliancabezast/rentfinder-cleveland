Renting a house near Gordon Square

Gordon Square is the walkable arts and entertainment district centered on Detroit Avenue between roughly W. 58th and W. 65th Streets, inside the larger Detroit-Shoreway neighborhood on Cleveland's near-west side (ZIP 44102). The district takes its name from the Gordon Square Arts District and is home to the Capitol Theatre, Near West Theatre, and Cleveland Public Theatre, along with restaurants and small shops along Detroit Avenue.

Houses for rent near Gordon Square are mostly early-1900s duplexes and single-family homes on the residential side streets just off Detroit Avenue, a short walk to the theaters and business strip. How much does it cost to rent a house near Gordon Square?

Citywide, a 2-bedroom home has a median rent of about $1,100 a month and a 3-bedroom about $1,350, per Zumper's rent report dated July 2026. Getting around Gordon Square

Gordon Square sits directly on GCRTA's Detroit Avenue bus corridor, with frequent service east into Downtown and west toward Lakewood. The West Shoreway (Route 2) is a short drive north, giving quick highway access along the Lake Erie shoreline, and Edgewater Park is about a mile north for outdoor recreation.

  • ZIP code: 44102 (part of the Detroit-Shoreway neighborhood)
  • Transit: Detroit Avenue bus routes; West Shoreway (Route 2) nearby
  • Arts district: Capitol Theatre, Near West Theatre, Cleveland Public Theatre
  • Green space: Edgewater Park about a mile north

Section 8 and vouchers near Gordon Square

Gordon Square falls within ZIP 44102, one of the areas where we currently work with rental homes, and every home we work with accepts Housing Choice Vouchers (Section 8) and is HUD-inspection-ready. Ohio has no statewide law requiring landlords to accept vouchers, and Cleveland does not currently have a citywide source-of-income protection ordinance, so it's worth confirming voucher acceptance with any landlord you contact — ours accept them on every unit.

Start an application if you've already found a home you like, or read our Section 8 guide for how CMHA's voucher process, waitlist, and inspections work locally. CMHA's own Housing Choice Voucher page has current program details.
