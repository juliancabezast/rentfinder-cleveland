Renting a house in Union-Miles

Union-Miles Park is a Statistical Planning Area on Cleveland's southeast side, named for the intersection of Union and Miles avenues that runs through the neighborhood. It sits alongside Slavic Village and shares ZIP code 44105 with that neighborhood, and borders the suburb of Garfield Heights to the south.

Transit and getting around Union-Miles

Union-Miles is served by RTA bus routes connecting to downtown Cleveland and to the southeast suburbs, and Interstate 77 runs along the area's western edge for drivers heading downtown or toward Garfield Heights and points south. The neighborhood is also a short drive from the Slavic Village business district along Broadway Avenue.

  • Side of city: Southeast
  • Highway access: Interstate 77

Section 8 vouchers in Union-Miles

Every home Rent Finder Cleveland works with — including in Union-Miles — accepts Section 8 and is HUD-inspection-ready. Vouchers are administered locally by the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA), which accepts preliminary applications year-round; read our Section 8 housing guide for the full process.

Keep in mind Ohio has no statewide source-of-income protection law, and the City of Cleveland has not enacted a voucher-nondiscrimination ordinance as of July 2026.
