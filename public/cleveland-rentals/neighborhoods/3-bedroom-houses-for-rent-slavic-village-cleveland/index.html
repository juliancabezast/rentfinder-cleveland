Slavic Village sits on Cleveland's southeast side along the Morgana Run Trail, split between the Broadway-Slavic Village and North Broadway areas within ZIP code 44105. Three-bedroom homes are one of the more available sizes here, generally offering a basement and a small yard alongside the extra bedroom, and the neighborhood is one of the city's more affordable for renters needing that additional space.

How much does a 3-bedroom house cost in Slavic Village?

Three-bedroom homes in the 44105 area commonly rent from about $1,000 to $1,250 a month, depending on condition, size, and whether utilities are included — among the more affordable 3-bedroom price points in the city. For comparison, Zumper's rent report dated July 4, 2026 puts Cleveland's overall citywide 3-bedroom median noticeably higher, at $1,350, and RentCafe's July 2, 2026 report on professionally managed apartments puts the 3-bedroom average far higher still, at $2,716, reflecting newer, larger buildings rather than the older individual houses typical of Slavic Village.

Across Rent Finder Cleveland's broader portfolio, 3-bedroom homes typically run from about $1,100 to $1,500 a month, so Slavic Village prices toward the lower end of what we manage citywide.

What Slavic Village's 3-bedroom rental homes are like

Most of the neighborhood's rental housing stock dates to the early 1900s, a mix of single-family homes, duplexes, and small multi-family buildings with front porches, full basements, and modest yards. Three-bedroom units in this stock generally come with the basement and yard, though exact features vary listing to listing — always confirm details for a specific address before applying. Getting around Slavic Village

Slavic Village is served by several RTA bus routes connecting to Downtown, and the neighborhood sits close to I-77 and I-490 for drivers. The Morgana Run Trail offers a paved path for walking and biking through the neighborhood, and the commercial strips along Broadway Avenue and Fleet Avenue cover everyday shopping without a long drive.

Section 8 and 3-bedroom vouchers in Slavic Village

Households that need a 3-bedroom home to meet CMHA's voucher size standard can apply for a Housing Choice Voucher year-round through the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority's online portal. CMHA's published income limit for a 4-person household, effective April 3, 2025, is $49,700, and selection then runs through periodic mini-lotteries rather than a simple waiting line.

Every 3-bedroom home Rent Finder Cleveland manages in Slavic Village accepts Section 8 vouchers and is kept HUD-inspection-ready, so voucher holders can tour and apply with confidence.
