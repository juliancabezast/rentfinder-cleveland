Transit and getting around Mount Pleasant

The neighborhood is served by RTA bus routes connecting to downtown Cleveland and to nearby Shaker Square, and it sits within a few miles of the Cleveland Clinic main campus in neighboring Fairfax. Kinsman Road is a main east-west corridor through the area.

  • Side of city: Southeast
  • Nearby institution: Cleveland Clinic main campus (Fairfax)
  • Transit: RTA bus routes to downtown and Shaker Square
  • Main corridor: Kinsman Road

Section 8 and vouchers in southeast Cleveland

Southeast Cleveland, including the area around Mount Pleasant, is served by the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA), the local Housing Choice Voucher administrator. CMHA accepts preliminary applications year-round, though placement is by lottery and can take months.

Does Ohio or Cleveland protect Section 8 voucher holders?

A handful of Cuyahoga County suburbs, including Cleveland Heights and South Euclid, do have local protections. Always confirm a landlord's policy directly.
