Getting around the West Park area

West Park is served by GCRTA bus routes running along Lorain Avenue and connecting into the wider regional transit network, and Cleveland Hopkins International Airport sits nearby on the city's far southwest side. Interstate 71 runs along Cleveland's west side, giving West Park renters a direct route toward Downtown's Tower City rail hub, where GCRTA's Red Line, Blue Line, Green Line, and Waterfront Line all converge.

Section 8 and voucher-friendly houses near Kamm's Corners

Housing Choice Vouchers (Section 8) are administered locally by the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA), which accepts preliminary voucher applications year-round through its online applicant portal. Ohio has no statewide law requiring landlords to accept vouchers, and the City of Cleveland itself does not have a source-of-income protection ordinance, so acceptance varies by landlord. Every home we manage is Section 8-friendly and HUD-inspection-ready — read more in our Cleveland Section 8 housing guide.
