Getting around from Puritas

Puritas sits near Cleveland Hopkins International Airport, which is also the western terminus of the RTA Red Line — the region's heavy-rail line running from the airport through downtown Tower City to Cleveland's East Side. Interstate 71 runs along the area's eastern edge, giving quick highway access toward downtown and the southern suburbs.

As with any address, confirm the closest bus routes and rail stop directly with Greater Cleveland RTA before choosing a home.

Section 8 vouchers if you're renting on the west side

Ohio has no statewide law requiring landlords to accept housing vouchers, and the City of Cleveland does not currently have a source-of-income protection ordinance — those protections exist only in a handful of Cuyahoga County suburbs, such as Cleveland Heights and South Euclid, as of July 2026. That means voucher acceptance varies by landlord and by area, including on the far west side.

Every home Rent Finder Cleveland works with accepts Section 8 / Housing Choice Vouchers and is HUD-inspection-ready. See our Section 8 housing guide for how CMHA's voucher program works and where our current inventory is concentrated.
