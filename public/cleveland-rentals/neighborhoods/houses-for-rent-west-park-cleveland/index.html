Houses for Rent in West Park, Cleveland

What is West Park, and where does it sit in Cleveland?

West Park is one of Cleveland's largest west-side residential areas, encompassing the smaller Jefferson and Kamm's Corners planning districts near the city's western border with Fairview Park and North Olmsted. The area developed in the early-to-mid 1900s as streetcar suburbs annexed into the city, which is why the housing stock leans toward brick and frame single-family bungalows and colonials on modest lots.

The district sits close to Cleveland Hopkins International Airport to the south and is bounded by the Rocky River valley (part of Cleveland Metroparks) to the west, giving it a more suburban feel than east-side neighborhoods closer to downtown.

How much does it cost to rent a house in West Park?

West Park doesn't have its own published rent index, so the closest grounded benchmark is citywide Cleveland data. Per Zumper's July 4, 2026 report, the Cleveland median asking rent is $1,250/month overall, with 2-bedroom units around $1,100 and 3-bedroom units around $1,350. RentCafe's July 2, 2026 report on professionally-managed apartments puts the citywide average considerably higher, at $1,564/month for 787 square feet, reflecting a different mix of newer buildings. West Park's older single-family housing stock tends to price closer to the Zumper end of that range.

For comparison, our own selection of rental homes across Cleveland runs from about $700 to $1,800 a month, with most homes in the $1,000 range — mostly 2- and 3-bedroom single-family houses, duplexes, triplexes, and fourplexes.

Getting around West Park

West Park is served by Greater Cleveland RTA bus routes running along Lorain Avenue, Puritas Avenue, and Rocky River Drive into downtown and connecting suburbs, per GCRTA's published route network. It's not on the Red Line, but riders can connect to rail service at nearby West Park or Triskett-area stops depending on the specific route. Interstate 71 and I-480 both run near the district, making it a straightforward drive to downtown, the airport, or western suburbs like North Olmsted and Westlake.

  • Location: Far west side, bordering Fairview Park and North Olmsted
  • Transit: GCRTA bus routes along Lorain Ave, Puritas Ave, and Rocky River Dr
  • Highways: Near I-71 and I-480; close to Cleveland Hopkins International Airport
  • Green space: Adjacent to the Rocky River Reservation (Cleveland Metroparks)
