What is the West Boulevard neighborhood?

West Boulevard is one of Cleveland's official near-west-side residential districts, named for West Boulevard, the wide street that runs through it connecting the Madison Avenue corridor near Lake Erie down toward the Puritas-Longmead and Riverside areas farther south. It sits generally within ZIP code 44102, bordered by Cudell and Detroit-Shoreway to the north.

Like much of Cleveland's near-west side, the neighborhood's housing stock dates mostly to the early-to-mid 1900s, with a mix of single-family frame houses and two-family duplexes on modest city lots.

How much does it cost to rent a house in West Boulevard?

There's no West Boulevard-specific rent index, so the closest grounded benchmark is citywide Cleveland data. Per Zumper's July 4, 2026 report, Cleveland's overall median asking rent is $1,250/month, with 2-bedroom units averaging around $1,100 and 3-bedroom units around $1,350. RentCafe's July 2, 2026 report on professionally-managed apartments citywide puts the average considerably higher, at $1,564/month for 787 square feet, reflecting a different mix of newer buildings rather than this corridor's older housing stock.

For comparison, Rent Finder Cleveland's own selection of rental homes across the city runs from about $700 to $1,800 a month, typically around $1,000, spanning single-family houses, duplexes, triplexes, and fourplexes.

Getting around West Boulevard

The GCRTA Red Line's West Boulevard-Cudell rapid station sits along the neighborhood's northern edge on Madison Avenue, offering direct rail access to downtown Cleveland's Tower City hub in one direction and Cleveland Hopkins International Airport in the other, per GCRTA's published rail network. Bus routes along Madison Avenue and Lorain Avenue also connect the area to downtown and to neighboring west-side districts, and I-90 (the Shoreway) is a short drive north.

  • Location: Near-west side, bordering Cudell and Detroit-Shoreway to the north
  • ZIP code: Generally 44102, extending toward 44111 near its southern edge
  • Transit: GCRTA Red Line (West Boulevard-Cudell station) plus Madison Ave and Lorain Ave bus routes
  • Highways: Short drive to I-90 (Shoreway)
