Renting a house in Willoughby, Ohio

Willoughby is a Lake County suburb roughly 20 miles northeast of downtown Cleveland, generally reached from the city via I-90, with most addresses falling in ZIP code 44094. It sits near several other Lake County lakefront communities — Eastlake, Willowick, and Wickliffe are all nearby to the northwest, while Mentor, the county's largest city, is a bit farther northeast.

Rent Finder Cleveland's 90+ managed rental homes are concentrated in Cleveland's east and southeast side neighborhoods, plus a few other Ohio cities — Willoughby is not currently part of our portfolio. If Willoughby specifically is a must, a Lake County-focused property manager or local listing service will have more direct options. If you're open to Cleveland-area neighborhoods instead, tell our team your bedroom count and budget and we'll show you what we currently manage.

One geographic detail worth knowing if you're weighing Willoughby against a Cleveland listing: Willoughby, like the rest of Lake County, sits within Northeast Ohio's lake-effect snow belt, which produces higher average seasonal snowfall than west-side lakefront communities. That's a function of prevailing winds off Lake Erie, not a comment on any neighborhood's desirability, but it's worth factoring into a winter commute if you're comparing the two areas.

What does it typically cost to rent in the Cleveland region?

Willoughby-specific rent data isn't published with a dated source, but as a general Northeast Ohio benchmark, Zumper's July 4, 2026 report shows a Cleveland median asking rent of $1,250/month, with 2-bedroom units around $1,100 and 3-bedroom units around $1,350. Getting around from Willoughby

Willoughby sits outside Greater Cleveland RTA's core Cuyahoga County bus network, so a personal vehicle is the practical way to reach downtown Cleveland, generally via I-90. The commute runs through Lake County's chain of lakefront suburbs — Eastlake and Willowick sit just to the northwest, with Wickliffe a bit farther southwest toward Cuyahoga County, and Mentor extending the corridor northeast.

  • ZIP code: 44094 (most of Willoughby)
  • Transit: Outside GCRTA's core Cuyahoga County bus network; reached via I-90 by car
  • Location: Lake County, roughly 20 miles northeast of downtown Cleveland
  • Climate note: Within Northeast Ohio's lake-effect snow belt, along with the rest of Lake County

Section 8 and voucher holders looking at Willoughby

The Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA) administers the Housing Choice Voucher program for Cuyahoga County, but Willoughby is in Lake County, outside CMHA's jurisdiction — a different local public housing authority handles voucher administration there, so check with Lake County's housing authority directly for waitlist and payment-standard details. What we can confirm about our own homes: every property Rent Finder Cleveland manages accepts Section 8 / Housing Choice Vouchers and is HUD-inspection-ready, though our current inventory sits in Cleveland, not Willoughby.
