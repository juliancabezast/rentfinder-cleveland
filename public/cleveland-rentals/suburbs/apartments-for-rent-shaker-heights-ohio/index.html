Getting around Shaker Heights

Shaker Heights is one of the few Cuyahoga County suburbs with its own rail stations: the RTA's Blue Line (Van Aken branch) and Green Line (Green Road branch) both run from downtown Cleveland's Tower City through Shaker Heights along Shaker Boulevard. Local RTA bus routes connect to University Circle and to the Buckeye-Shaker area of Cleveland just across the line.

  • County: Cuyahoga County, east/southeast of Cleveland
  • Borders: Cleveland's Buckeye-Shaker neighborhood (ZIP 44120)
  • Transit: RTA Blue Line (Van Aken) and Green Line (Green Rd) light rail to Tower City downtown
  • Districts: Shaker Square and the Van Aken District

Section 8 vouchers near Shaker Heights

Housing Choice Vouchers are administered countywide through CMHA, and every home Rent Finder Cleveland works with — including our rentals just across the line in Buckeye-Shaker — accepts vouchers and is HUD-inspection-ready. Ohio has no statewide source-of-income law, so check directly with any Shaker Heights landlord about their voucher policy; see our guide to Cleveland-area source-of-income rules for which local municipalities currently ban refusing a voucher outright.
