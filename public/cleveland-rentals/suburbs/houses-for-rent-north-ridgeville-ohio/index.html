Renting a house in North Ridgeville, Ohio

North Ridgeville sits in Lorain County, west/southwest of Cleveland, bordering North Olmsted in Cuyahoga County to the east and Avon to the north, with ZIP code 44039 covering the city. Interstate 480 runs to its western terminus in North Ridgeville, connecting to State Route 10 (the Lorain County Freeway), which continues west toward Elyria. Housing stock is a mix of older farmhouses on larger parcels and newer single-family subdivisions built as development has extended west from the Cuyahoga County line.

Getting around North Ridgeville

North Ridgeville is served by Interstate 480, which ends at its western terminus in the city and connects to State Route 10, the main freeway running west toward Elyria and Lorain. State Route 20 (Center Ridge Road) also runs through the city as a local east-west corridor. Greater Cleveland RTA's fixed bus network does not extend this far west into Lorain County, so a car is the practical way to get around North Ridgeville and commute toward Cleveland or the rest of Lorain County.

Section 8 and voucher holders looking in North Ridgeville

North Ridgeville is in Lorain County rather than Cuyahoga County, so the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA) Housing Choice Voucher program described in our Cleveland-area guides does not directly administer vouchers here — renters in North Ridgeville should check with Lorain County's local public housing authority for the applicable voucher process. Ohio has no statewide law requiring landlords to accept Housing Choice Vouchers, so acceptance in North Ridgeville depends on the individual landlord regardless of county.
