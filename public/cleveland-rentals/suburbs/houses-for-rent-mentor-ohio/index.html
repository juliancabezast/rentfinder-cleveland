Where Mentor, Ohio is located

Mentor is a Lake County city roughly 25 miles northeast of Cleveland along the I-90 corridor, and it's the largest city in Lake County. It sits on Lake Erie, near Headlands Beach State Park, and includes the Great Lakes Mall commercial corridor along Route 615/Mentor Avenue.

Housing in Mentor spans postwar single-family subdivisions closer to the lake to newer construction further from the shoreline, which is typical of Lake County's suburban growth pattern since the mid-20th century.

How Section 8 vouchers work if you're searching in Mentor

Mentor is in Lake County, not Cuyahoga County, so the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA) — the agency that administers vouchers for Cleveland and Cuyahoga County — is not the agency covering Mentor. Housing Choice Vouchers nationwide are run locally by the public housing authority (PHA) with jurisdiction over a given address; HUD's Housing Choice Voucher program overview explains how the program works and can help you identify the correct PHA for a Lake County address.

Ohio has no statewide law requiring a landlord to accept a voucher as a source of income, and no local ordinance covering Mentor was identified in this review — confirm directly with the city and with a landlord before assuming voucher acceptance. Every home Rent Finder Cleveland works with accepts Section 8 and is HUD-inspection-ready; see our Cleveland Section 8 guide for how that works for our own selection.

Ohio landlord-tenant law that applies in Mentor

Ohio's landlord-tenant statute is state law and applies in Mentor the same as anywhere else in Ohio. Under ORC 5321.16, a security deposit over $50 or one month's rent (whichever is greater) earns 5% annual interest once a tenant stays six months or more, and must be returned with an itemized statement within 30 days of move-out. Before an eviction filing for nonpayment, a landlord must serve a statutory 3-day notice under ORC 1923.04. This is general information, not legal advice — verify current statute language before relying on it.
