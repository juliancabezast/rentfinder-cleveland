Cleveland-area rent benchmarks for Eastlake renters

We don't operate in Eastlake, so there's no Eastlake-specific figure to quote, but the wider Cleveland market gives a useful reference point for anyone comparing Lake County options against the city. Zumper's rent research, dated July 4, 2026, puts the Cleveland-area median asking rent at $1,250 a month, a 4.2% increase year-over-year. RentCafe's (Yardi) report, dated July 2, 2026, puts the average rent across professionally managed Cleveland-area apartments at $1,564 a month for roughly 787 square feet.

The gap between those two figures comes down to methodology: RentCafe's average leans toward larger, newer managed buildings, while Zumper's median reflects the broader mix of listings, including older and smaller units. Either way, both sources place Cleveland-area rent well under the $1,950 national median Zumper reports — about 36% lower.

Housing vouchers and Section 8 near Eastlake

Housing Choice Vouchers (Section 8) for Cuyahoga County are administered by the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA). Eastlake is in Lake County, outside CMHA's jurisdiction, so a voucher holder looking to rent there should ask their caseworker which public housing authority actually covers Lake County addresses and what the port-in process looks like before assuming CMHA rules apply.

It's also worth knowing that Ohio has no statewide law requiring a landlord to accept a housing voucher as payment. As of our July 2026 research, source-of-income protections for voucher holders exist only in a small set of Cuyahoga County suburbs — Cleveland Heights, South Euclid, University Heights, Warrensville Heights, and Linndale — and none of our sources show a comparable ordinance in Eastlake or elsewhere in Lake County. Confirm current local rules directly before counting on a specific address.

Every home Rent Finder Cleveland manages accepts Section 8 and is HUD-inspection-ready, without exception — that guarantee just doesn't currently extend to a Lake County address. See our Section 8 housing guide for Cleveland for how the process works where we do operate.
