Does Rent Finder Cleveland rent rooms near Case Western?

Not as single rooms. We work with whole rental units — houses, duplexes, triplexes, and fourplexes — leased to one household at a time, not individual bedrooms in a shared student house. If you're specifically after a private room with a shared kitchen and common areas, our model won't match that search.

What we can offer is honest: some of our Cleveland rental homes sit in Hough and Glenville, the neighborhoods immediately bordering University Circle, where Case Western Reserve University, the Cleveland Clinic main campus, and Severance Music Center are located. A group of roommates, a graduate student household, or hospital/university staff can lease one of these multi-bedroom homes together.

Where our nearest homes sit relative to University Circle

Hough sits on Cleveland's east side, between Downtown and University Circle, while Glenville is on the northeast side, bordering University Circle and St. Clair Avenue. Both neighborhoods are within our current service area — Hough (ZIP 44103) and Glenville (ZIP 44108) together account for a meaningful share of our Cleveland rental homes.

University Circle itself is served by the RTA Red Line (heavy rail) and the HealthLine, a bus-rapid-transit route running along Euclid Avenue between Public Square, University Circle, and East Cleveland. Renters in Hough and Glenville can reach University Circle by bus or by connecting to these lines rather than needing a car for every trip to campus or the medical campuses.

What renting near a college and hospital corridor typically costs

Rent near a university and hospital corridor tends to run above the citywide median because more of the nearby housing stock is professionally managed. Two dated sources illustrate the range: Zumper's Cleveland report (July 4, 2026) puts the citywide median at $1,250/month across all sizes, while RentCafe's report (July 2, 2026) — which indexes larger, professionally-managed apartment buildings, more common close to University Circle — shows meaningfully higher averages.

Frequently asked questions

Does Rent Finder Cleveland lease individual rooms near Case Western?
No. We lease whole rental units, not individual rooms. We do work with 2- and 3-bedroom homes in Hough and Glenville, the neighborhoods bordering University Circle, which can work for a roommate group or small household leasing together.
How do I get from Hough or Glenville to University Circle without a car?
University Circle is served by the RTA Red Line and the HealthLine bus-rapid-transit route along Euclid Avenue, connecting Public Square, University Circle, and East Cleveland. Renters in Hough and Glenville can reach campus and the Cleveland Clinic area using these connections.
Is renting near University Circle more expensive than the rest of Cleveland?
Often, yes, for professionally-managed apartment buildings. RentCafe's July 2026 report shows managed-apartment averages of $1,451 for a 1-bedroom and $1,818 for a 2-bedroom, well above Zumper's citywide median of $1,195 and $1,100 for the same sizes.
Can students use a Section 8 voucher for housing near Case Western?
Every home Rent Finder Cleveland works with accepts Housing Choice Vouchers and is HUD-inspection-ready. Voucher eligibility for students has additional federal rules, so confirm student-specific eligibility directly with CMHA before applying.
What's the best way to find a rental home near campus through Rent Finder Cleveland?
Tell our team your budget, target move-in date, and how many people will be on the lease. We'll let you know which current or upcoming homes in Hough, Glenville, or other nearby neighborhoods realistically fit, rather than pointing you to inventory we don't have.
Are Rent Finder Cleveland homes near University Circle furnished for students?
No. Our rental homes are standard unfurnished residential units, the same as the rest of our available homes. If you need furniture, plan to bring your own or rent it separately — we don't offer a furnished, semester-length student housing product.
