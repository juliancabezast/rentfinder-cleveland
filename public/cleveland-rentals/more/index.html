Frequently asked questions

Do you rent rooms or short-term stays?
Our focus is longer-term residential rental homes, not single rooms or short-term stays. These guides explain how those markets work in Cleveland and where to start. If your need overlaps with a longer-term rental home — including furnished or family-sized options — tell us your criteria and we'll help point you in the right direction.
Where do students find housing near Cleveland universities?
Off-campus rentals near University Circle (Case Western Reserve) and downtown (Cleveland State) are common, along with rooms and shared houses. Our student housing guide covers the basics. If you want a longer-term house or apartment near campus, our standard neighborhood and houses guides may fit better, and every home we work with accepts vouchers.
Can you help with senior housing?
Senior housing ranges from independent living to income-based and assisted options, and much of it runs through specialized providers. Our senior housing guide points you toward the right resources. Where your need is simply a long-term, ground-floor or accessible rental home, we may be able to help — tell us your requirements.
I need housing urgently — can you help same day?
We can't promise same-day placement; live availability changes constantly and every rental involves screening and, for vouchers, inspection. What we can do is help you understand your options and match you to a longer-term voucher-friendly home if one fits. Our emergency and low-income housing guides point toward local resources for urgent needs.

Looking for a rental in this area?

Tell us what you need and we'll show you what's available now. We work with 90+ voucher-friendly homes across the Cleveland area and add more regularly.
