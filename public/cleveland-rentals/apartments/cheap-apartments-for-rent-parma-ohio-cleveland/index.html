Parma's location and nearby Cleveland neighborhoods

Parma sits just south of Cleveland's Old Brooklyn neighborhood, near the Cleveland Metroparks Zoo, and Brooklyn Centre, which lies just south of the Cuyahoga Valley. These two neighborhoods form Parma's northern border with the city.

GCRTA runs bus routes through Parma connecting to Downtown Cleveland and the Tower City rail hub, where the Red, Blue, Green, and Waterfront lines all meet for onward transit around the region.

Section 8 and voucher-friendly apartments near Parma

Ohio has no statewide source-of-income protection law, so a landlord's acceptance of a Housing Choice Voucher depends on local ordinance or individual policy. As of July 2026, the reported Cuyahoga County suburbs with a local voucher-holder protection ordinance are Cleveland Heights, South Euclid, University Heights, Warrensville Heights, and Linndale — Parma is not currently on that list, so acceptance there depends on the individual landlord; renters should verify directly.

Every rental home Rent Finder Cleveland manages accepts Housing Choice Vouchers and is HUD-inspection-ready, including our homes near Parma in the 44109 ZIP. Vouchers in Cuyahoga County are administered by the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA); see our Section 8 apartments near Parma guide for more detail on applying and waitlists.

Tips for finding a cheap apartment near Parma

When comparing prices, look at both Zumper's median-asking figures and RentCafe's professionally-managed averages side by side — the two sources track different segments of the market, so a single 'average rent' figure can be misleading on its own. Widening your search a few blocks north into Cleveland's Old Brooklyn and Brooklyn Centre neighborhoods can surface more voucher-friendly options in a similar price range.

If a Housing Choice Voucher is part of your plan, start the CMHA application alongside your apartment search — preliminary intake runs year-round with no closing date, but placement is by random lottery and can take anywhere from a few days to several months.
