Apartment rents in North Olmsted, Ohio

North Olmsted is a Cuyahoga County suburb positioned west/southwest of Cleveland, bordering Fairview Park and Olmsted Falls/Olmsted Township, and near Cleveland Hopkins International Airport. It's a well-established residential suburb with a mix of mid-century garden-style apartment complexes, particularly along Lorain Rd and Great Northern Blvd near the Great Northern Mall corridor, plus single-family rental homes on quieter side streets.

There's no independently published North Olmsted-only apartment rent index, so the closest reliable comparison is the Greater Cleveland metro-area benchmark below; individual complex pricing in North Olmsted varies with unit age, amenities, and proximity to Great Northern Mall or the airport.

Is North Olmsted actually cheap compared to other cities?

Greater Cleveland as a whole is one of the more affordable major metro rental markets in the country: Zumper's July 4, 2026 report shows the region's overall median rent running about 35.9% below the $1,950/month national median. That affordability extends to west-side suburbs like North Olmsted, which sit along the same I-480 corridor as several lower-cost west-side Cleveland neighborhoods.

Within North Olmsted specifically, the mid-century garden-apartment complexes along Lorain Rd and Great Northern Blvd tend to price toward the lower end of the Cleveland-metro range, while newer or renovated units can run closer to the higher RentCafe averages shown above.

Getting around North Olmsted

North Olmsted is served by GCRTA bus routes connecting to Downtown Cleveland and to nearby Cleveland Hopkins International Airport, which also serves as the western terminus of the RTA Red Line rapid-transit route. The suburb sits along the I-480 corridor, making it a straightforward drive into Cleveland's west side or Downtown.

Section 8 and housing vouchers near North Olmsted

Ohio has no statewide source-of-income protection law, so Section 8 acceptance in North Olmsted depends on the individual landlord's policy — North Olmsted is not currently reported among the Cuyahoga County suburbs (Cleveland Heights, South Euclid, University Heights, Warrensville Heights, Linndale) with a local voucher-holder protection ordinance as of July 2026.
