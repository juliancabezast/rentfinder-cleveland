Euclid's location and nearby Cleveland neighborhoods

Euclid directly borders Cleveland's Euclid-Green neighborhood, the city's far-northeast corner, and sits just north of Collinwood-Nottingham, both along the same stretch of Lake Erie shoreline. Nearby North Shore Collinwood, home to the Waterloo Arts District, shares the same lakefront setting.

GCRTA runs bus service through Euclid connecting to Downtown Cleveland and the Tower City rail hub, where the Red, Blue, Green, and Waterfront lines converge for onward transit.

Section 8 and voucher-friendly apartments near Euclid

Ohio has no statewide source-of-income protection law, so whether a landlord accepts a Housing Choice Voucher depends on local ordinance or individual policy. As of July 2026, the reported Cuyahoga County suburbs with a local voucher-holder protection ordinance are Cleveland Heights, South Euclid, University Heights, Warrensville Heights, and Linndale. Note that Euclid and South Euclid are two separate cities — Euclid itself is not currently reported on that protected list, so acceptance there depends on the individual landlord; renters should verify directly.

Every rental home Rent Finder Cleveland works with accepts Housing Choice Vouchers and is HUD-inspection-ready, including our homes near Euclid in Collinwood. Vouchers in Cuyahoga County are administered by the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA); see our Section 8 apartments near Euclid guide for details on applying and waitlists.

Tips for finding a cheap apartment near Euclid

When comparing prices, check both Zumper's median-asking figures and RentCafe's professionally-managed averages — the gap between the two mostly reflects older lakefront buildings versus newer managed complexes, not a pricing error. Widening your search a few blocks south into Cleveland's Collinwood-Nottingham neighborhood can surface more voucher-friendly options along the same Lake Erie corridor.

If a Housing Choice Voucher is part of your plan, start the CMHA application alongside your apartment search — preliminary intake runs year-round with no closing date, but placement is by random lottery and can take anywhere from a few days to several months.
